Rules:
  1. Dealer sets number of draws and discard limit.
  2. 5 cards are dealt to each player.
  3. Betting round starts with player to left of dealer.
  4. Players discard and are dealt replacement cards.
  5. Betting round starts with next player in rotation.
  6. Repeat until the number of draws allowed is reached.
  7. Highest 5 card hand wins.

7 Card
Rules:
  1. Wild cards are 3's and 9's.
  2. Players dealt a 4 face up, are dealt an extra card face up.
  3. Players must pay for any dealt face up 3's, 9's and 4's.
  4. 2 cards face down are dealt to each player.
  5. Betting round starts with player to left of dealer.
  6. Repeat (4x) 1 card face up dealt to each player with a betting round. Betting starts with highest hand showing.
  7. Final card is dealt face down to each player with a final betting round.
  8. Players will have a total of 7 cards plus any additional cards from a face up 4.
  9. Highest 5 card hand wins.
7 Card
Rules:
  1. Wild cards are 3's and 9's.
  2. 4's give a player an extra card face down.
  3. Players must pay for any turned up 3's, 9's and 4's.
  4. 9 or 7 cards face down are dealt to each player.
  5. Players must not look at their cards.
  6. The player left of the dealer starts by turning up a card from their pile. The player can then start a betting round or check.
  7. The next player in rotation turns over a card and continues turning cards until they have a higher hand then the previous player. The player can then start a betting round.
  8. This is repeated for each player in rotation until no cards remain, the player must have a higher hand then the current high hand on the table. If the player does not have a higher hand after they have turned over all their cards, they must fold.
  9. Highest 5 card hand wins.
7 Card 9 Card
Rules:
  1. A seven card stud game with the pot split between the highest hand and the highest spade in the hole (face up spades do not count for the highest spade).
  2. 2 cards face down and 1 card face up are dealt to each player.
  3. Betting round starts with player with highest hand showing.
  4. Repeat (3x) 1 card face up dealt to each player and betting round.
  5. Final card is dealt face down with a final betting round.
  6. Highest 5 card hand and highest face down spade split the pot.

Variation: After final betting round, active players declare if they are going for highest (face down) spade, highest hand or both. A player declaring both wins only if holding the highest spade and the highest hand.

Community
Rules:
  1. Dealer sets number of draws and discard limit.
  2. A player must have Jacks or Better in their first hand to open.
  3. 5 cards face down are dealt to each player.
  4. Betting round starts with player to left of dealer and in rotation. Players that do not have Jack's or Better can not open betting and it moves to the next player in rotation. If a player can open, they start the betting round and the remaining players must equal or raise the bet to stay in.
  5. If no player can open the first round then the game changes to lowball.
  6. Players discard and are dealt replacement cards with betting rounds.
  7. Highest 5 card hand wins if a player can open.
  8. Lowest 5 card hand wins if no player can open.
5 Card
Rules:
  1. Dealer sets number of draws and discard limit.
  2. 5 cards face down are dealt to each player.
  3. Betting round begins with player to left of dealer. Player must have Jacks or Better to open the betting, if they do not it moves to the next player in rotation. If a player opens the betting round, the game proceeds the same as 5 card draw.
  4. If no player can open, the cards are thrown in and a new hand is dealt with the opening requirement moving up to Queens or Better. This process continues until Aces or better are required and no player can open. At this point the cards are thrown in and redealt and the game changes to lowball.
  5. Players discard and are dealt replacment cards with betting rounds.
  6. Highest 5 card hand wins if a player can open.
  7. Lowest 5 card hand wins if no player can open.

7 Card
Rules:
  1. 2 cards face down and 1 card face up are dealt to each player.
  2. Betting round starting with highest showing hand.
  3. The deck is then placed in the middle of the table and 2 cards from the deck are turned up on either side.
  4. Repeat (3x) Each player in turn selects a card, either one of the face up cards or a card from the top of the deck. The selected card is placed face up. As face up cards are removed from the deck area, they are replaced with a card from the top of the deck. Betting round starting with the highest showing hand.
  5. Final card is selected, either one of the face up or top deck card. If a face up card is selected it is placed face up. If the top deck card is selected it is placed face down. As face up cards are removed from the deck area, they are replaced with a card from the top of the deck. After all players have 7 cards, there will either be 1 or 2 face up deck cards remaining, dependig whether the last card taken is a face up or top deck card. These central cards are out of play, but known to the players during the final betting round.
  6. Highest 5 card hand wins.

7 Card
Rules:
  1. 2 face down cards are dealt to each player and 9 face down community cards in 3x3 grid on the table.
  2. Betting round starting with player to left of dealer.
  3. 4 corner community cards are turned over with betting round.
  4. 4 cards between the corner cards (not center card) are turned over with betting round.
  5. Center community card is turned up and final betting round.
  6. Players must use the 2 cards from their hands and 3 community cards that are in a row, column or diagonal to make a 5 card hand.
  7. Highest 5 card hand wins.

Variaion 1: 4 cards can be dealt to each player rather than 2. Player may use 2, 3, or all 4 cards from their hands together with 3, 2 or 1 cards from any row, column or diagonal.

Variaion 2: community cards can be turned over one a time with betting each time.

Variation 3: Center community card is wild and cards of the same rank.
